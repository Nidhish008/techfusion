What is a mental health consultant?

A mental health consultant is a professional who provides guidance, support, and expertise to individuals, organizations, or communities regarding mental health-related issues. They may offer services such as counseling, psychoeducation, training, and program development to promote mental well-being and address challenges such as stress, anxiety, depression, trauma, and interpersonal conflicts. Mental health consultants often work collaboratively with clients to develop strategies for coping, healing, and improving overall mental health and quality of life.
